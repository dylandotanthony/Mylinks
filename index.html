🥖 Dylan’s HomoMade Goods — Bread Care

Stay crusty.

🥯 Bagels & English Muffs:
Keep in the bread bag 1 day, then move to a loosely closed plastic bag.

🍞 Loaves:
Store in the bread bag 1–2 days. Once cut, transfer to a loosely closed plastic bag. Sourdough stays fresh about 4–5 days at room temp.

✨ Fresh inclusions:
Freeze after 2–3 days once cut for best quality.

❄️ Freezing:
Wrap tightly in plastic wrap, then place in a freezer bag. Slice before freezing for convenience.

🔥 Refresh:
Lightly mist and bake at 350°F for 8–12 minutes. Toast slices as needed.

🚫 Avoid refrigeration — it speeds up staling.

💧 Dry bread?
Lightly moisten, wrap in foil, and warm to revive.
